Why JavaScript?

JS is super popular

  • Making visual and interactive stuff in the browser is fun!
  • Take the programming concepts and use them to make stuff in the browser
  • JS is used on frontend and the backend (node.js). So you can take your skills where you find interest
  • Very active community from all over the world
  • Low barrier to entry, common to hire without CS degree

What we teach?

  • JS/HTML/CSS are always used together
  • How to build your own fully functional web-page.
  • Past students have built quiz games, twitter clones, and other interactive projects
  • Self education, how to take what you have learned and keep going
  • Coding Collaboration
  • Clean code principals
  • Confidence about what you are doing

Reasons to join us?

  • Good opportunity to learn from professions
  • Volunteers are motivated to help you succeed
  • Build programming skills that apply to any language
  • Perfect first step into your career high demand industry

What can you expect afterwards?

  • Be familiar with basic programming principals
  • Possibility to join advanced class/course of Frontend Development
  • Ability to continue to learn on your own
  • With skills built here and self learning, likely to fit for an internship or a entry level job

Requirements

  • Know what the HTML/CSS is and how to use it
  • Basic computer skills
  • Motivation
