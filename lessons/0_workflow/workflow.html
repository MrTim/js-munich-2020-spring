Workflow

Steps to upload your homework

  • Create a NEW branch for your homework.

    • git branch homework-two

  • Do your assignment on your homework branch.
  • Push your changes in that branch.
  • Create a pull request to merge that branch into master.
  • Select ALL teachers as reviewer.
  • Reviewers going to add comments (if they find things to improve).

Steps to merge in master your homework

  • Do your new changes on your homework branch.
  • Push your new changes and let reviewers know you updated the code adding a comment.
  • They go through and resolve their comments.
  • When you get an approval comment (at least one of the teachers), you can merge your pull request in master.
